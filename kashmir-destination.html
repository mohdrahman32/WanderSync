Why Kashmir?

Known as the 'Paradise on Earth,' Kashmir mesmerizes with its lush valleys, pristine lakes, and Mughal gardens. From the tranquility of Srinagar’s Dal Lake to the adventure of Gulmarg’s ski slopes, this destination offers something for every traveler.

Top Highlights of Kashmir

Dal Lake

Sail on a shikara through serene waters surrounded by floating gardens.

Gulmarg

A winter wonderland with skiing and the world’s highest cable car.

Shalimar Bagh

A Mughal garden with terraced lawns and stunning fountains.

Pahalgam

A picturesque valley perfect for trekking and nature lovers.

Sonamarg

The 'Meadow of Gold' with golden meadows and glacier views.

Travel Recommendations

Hotels

  • The LaLiT Grand Palace Srinagar - Luxurious stay with Dal Lake views.
  • Vivanta Kashmir - Rich amenities in a scenic setting.
  • Radisson Srinagar - Moderate comfort near city center.
  • Hotel New Mamta - Budget-friendly with basic amenities.
  • Houseboats on Dal Lake - Unique budget stay on water.

Restaurants

  • Mughal Darbar - Rich Kashmiri Wazwan cuisine.
  • Chinar at The LaLiT - Upscale dining with local flavors.
  • Shamyana Restaurant - Moderate prices, good variety.
  • Krishna Dhaba - Budget-friendly local eats.
  • Ahdoo’s - Affordable Kashmiri specialties.

Spots

  • Dal Lake - Iconic shikara rides and serene beauty.
  • Gulmarg - Skiing and breathtaking views.
  • Shalimar Bagh - Historic Mughal garden.
  • Pahalgam - Scenic valley for trekking.
  • Sonamarg - Golden meadows and glaciers.
  • Nishat Bagh - Another stunning Mughal garden.
  • Shankaracharya Temple - Hilltop spiritual site.
  • Betaab Valley - Lush greenery from Bollywood fame.

Travel Tips for Kashmir

  • Best Time to Visit: October to March for snow, April to June for blooms.
  • Pack Warm: Even summers can be chilly in higher altitudes.
  • Local Transport: Shikaras and taxis are common; book in advance.
  • Stay Connected: Prepaid SIMs may not work; rely on hotel Wi-Fi.
