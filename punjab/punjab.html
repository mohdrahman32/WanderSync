About Punjab

Punjab, located in northern India, is known for its rich culture, fertile plains, and Sikh heritage. Established as a state on November 1, 1966, it features attractions like Amritsar and Chandigarh.

  • Capital: Chandigarh
  • Population: 30 million (approx.)
  • Language: Punjabi
  • Area: 50,362 sq km
  • Established: November 1, 1966
